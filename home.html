WHO ARE WE?

My dream came true when I constructed my own café, fulfilling my lifelong ambition of opening a coffee shop that would captivate and delight customers. It all began when I was assigned the task of creating a 3D model for my architecture class, and it was a no-brainer for me to choose to construct a café called "Kapehan Sa South." Little did I know that a few years later, destiny would bring me together with my girlfriend, who shared the same passion for exploring different cafes and indulging in the pleasure of coffee. Inspired by our mutual love for coffee and the desire to share that joy with others, we contemplated the idea of starting a cafe ourselves. However, being new to the business realm, we had to embark on a humble journey of knowledge acquisition, starting from scratch and eagerly absorbing every bit of information we could find. As an architect, I drew upon my expertise and renamed the café in alignment with my architectural company, "A.M. Design and Build," infusing the essence of our shared passion and vocation into the café's very identity. Through dedication and perseverance, we meticulously crafted a warm and inviting atmosphere at "Kapehan sa South," where coffee aficionados can savor every sip, enveloped in the welcoming embrace of our carefully curated ambiance and menu. Opening this café has been the realization of my dreams, a testament to the power of passion and the fulfillment that comes with transforming aspirations into reality.
